1. Astronomical calculation engine

SWISS EPHEMERIS Inside. Jyotish Vani uses the Swiss Ephemeris under a paid commercial Swiss Ephemeris Professional Edition license held by Gregforge Labs LLC. Copyright notices contained in the Swiss Ephemeris source and data files are preserved without modification by the App. Swiss Ephemeris is a registered trademark of its proprietor.

All astronomical positions used by Jyotish Vani are computed by the Swiss Ephemeris library. This includes planetary longitudes, ayanāṁśa values, ascendant and house cusps, eclipse data, nakṣatra and tithi boundaries, and other ephemeris-derived quantities. Jyotish Vani applies classical Vedic interpretive rules on top of those calculations; the calculations themselves are the work of the Swiss Ephemeris project.

4. Cultural and textual sources

Interpretive content in Jyotish Vani is informed by the classical Indian Jyotish literature, including (without limitation) Bṛhat Parāśara Horā Śāstra, Phaladīpikā, Saravali, Jātaka Pārijāta, Bṛhat Jātaka, Jaimini Sūtras, Muhūrta Cintāmaṇi, Muhūrta Mārtāṇḍa, and Vāstu Śāstra works. These texts are in the public domain. English renderings and modern restatements within the App are original work of Gregforge Labs LLC and its contributors.
