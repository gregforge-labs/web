This document supplements the Terms of Service of Gregforge Labs LLC ("Gregforge") and explains how paid features, subscriptions, cancellations, and refunds work in the Jyotish Vani app (the "App"). Paid features may vary by country, platform, account state, app version, and provider availability.

1. How Purchases Work

Any paid feature, consumable, or subscription in the App is sold and billed through the purchase channel shown at checkout, usually Google Play Billing on Android or Apple In-App Purchase on iOS. The platform collects payment from your account on file and remits the relevant amount to Gregforge. Your payment relationship is between you and the platform; we do not store your full credit-card number.

2. Subscription Basics

  • Auto-renewal. Subscriptions automatically renew at the end of each billing period (monthly, yearly, or as otherwise stated at the point of purchase) unless you cancel before the renewal date.
  • Price changes. We may change subscription prices. Where required by law or store policy, you will be notified in advance and given the option to cancel before the new price takes effect.
  • Trials. Where a free trial is offered, the subscription begins automatically at the end of the trial unless you cancel beforehand. You can use a trial only once per account.
  • Downgrades and upgrades. Changing tiers takes effect either immediately, with proration where supported, or at the next renewal depending on the platform and product rules.
  • Provider-dependent features. Some paid benefits may depend on third-party AI, cloud, app-store, or data providers. If a provider changes, becomes unavailable, or changes terms, we may replace, modify, limit, or discontinue affected features.

3. How to Cancel

You can cancel a subscription at any time. Cancellation stops future renewals; you retain access to paid features until the end of the current billing period.

  • Android (Google Play). Open Google Play → tap your profile → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions → select Jyotish Vani → Cancel subscription. Or visit play.google.com/store/account/subscriptions.
  • iOS (Apple). Open Settings → tap your name → Subscriptions → select Jyotish Vani → Cancel Subscription.

Uninstalling the App alone does not cancel your subscription.

The App settings page should provide a direct subscription-management entry when paid subscriptions are active and the platform supports it.

For purchases made directly through us outside the stores, refunds are considered case by case; contact support@gregforgelabs.com. Except where required by law or platform policy, refunds are not available for billing periods already consumed.
