What to include

  • Your Jyotish Vani account email address or phone number, if you have one.
  • Your device platform: iOS or Android.
  • The feature involved, such as login, Vani chat, subscription, notifications, speech dictation, compatibility, Panchanga, or Kundli.
  • A short description of what happened and any error message shown in the App.

Common support paths

  • Account and login. Contact us if email, phone, Google Sign-In, or Apple Sign-In does not work after you have checked your network connection and app version.
  • Subscriptions. Purchases, cancellations, billing, and refunds are handled by the Apple App Store or Google Play. See our Refund & Subscription Terms for details.
  • Restore purchases. Use Restore purchases in the App. If your paid access still does not appear, send us the platform and the store account used for the purchase; do not send payment-card details.
  • Account deletion. Use Delete Account in the App, or visit Account & Data Deletion if you cannot access the App.
  • Privacy requests. See the Privacy Policy for access, correction, deletion, opt-out, and regional privacy rights.
