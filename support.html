Support — Jyotish Vani

Last updated: June 20, 2026

For help with Jyotish Vani, email support@gregforgelabs.com. We review support requests in English and respond as soon as reasonably possible. Many common questions are answered on the FAQ page.

Email support Delete account

What to include

  • The phone number on your Jyotish Vani account, if you have one.
  • Your device platform: iOS or Android.
  • The feature involved, such as login, Vani chat, subscription, notifications, speech dictation, compatibility, Panchanga, or Kundli.
  • A short description of what happened and any error message shown in the App.

Common support paths

  • Questions about the app. Our FAQ covers Kundli, Panchang, Match compatibility, the Vani chat, subscriptions, privacy, and account deletion.
  • Account and login. Contact us if phone sign-in does not work after you have checked your network connection and app version.
  • Subscriptions. Purchases, cancellations, billing, and refunds are handled by the Apple App Store or Google Play. See our Refund & Subscription Terms for details.
  • Restore purchases. Use Restore purchases in the App. If your paid access still does not appear, send us the platform and the store account used for the purchase; do not send payment-card details.
  • Account deletion. Use Delete Account in the App, or visit Account & Data Deletion if you cannot access the App.
  • Privacy requests. See the Privacy Policy for access, correction, deletion, opt-out, and regional privacy rights.

Important notes

Jyotish Vani provides educational and entertainment astrology content. It is not medical, legal, financial, mental health, religious, or other professional advice. See the Disclaimer.

Do not email sensitive birth details, payment-card details, one-time verification codes, passwords, or private keys. If we need to verify account ownership, we will ask only for the minimum information required.
