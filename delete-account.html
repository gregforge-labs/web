Account & Data Deletion — Jyotish Vani

Last updated: April 17, 2026

You can request deletion of your Jyotish Vani account and associated data at any time.

How to request deletion

  1. Open Jyotish Vani and go to Settings.
  2. Choose Delete Account.
  3. Send the pre-filled email from the address registered to your account.

If you cannot access the App, send an email to support@gregforgelabs.com with the subject line Jyotish Vani Account Deletion Request. Include the email address associated with your Jyotish Vani account.

Request deletion by email Read Privacy Policy

What is deleted

  • Your Firebase Authentication account record associated with Jyotish Vani.
  • Any server-side account data we maintain for support, abuse prevention, billing access, or service operations, unless retention is legally required.

What is stored on your device

Your birth profiles, current location setting, and chat history are stored locally on your device. You can remove them by deleting the App, clearing app data, or using in-app clearing controls where available.

Timing

We will confirm receipt and complete eligible deletion requests within 7 days. Some records may be retained if required for security, fraud prevention, dispute resolution, tax, accounting, or legal compliance.
