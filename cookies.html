Cookies & Web Tracking

Last updated: May 19, 2026

This page describes cookies, local storage, and similar tracking technologies used by this Gregforge Labs website (the static pages hosted at gregforgelabs.com). It does not describe in-app tracking inside the Jyotish Vani mobile application; for that, see Privacy Policy.

1. Website cookies

This site is a set of static HTML pages hosted on a third-party static-hosting provider (currently GitHub Pages). We do not place first-party analytics, advertising, or personalization cookies on this site. We do not embed Google Analytics, Meta Pixel, TikTok Pixel, LinkedIn Insight, or any other behavioral-tracking script.

2. Operational cookies our host may set

The static-hosting provider may set strictly necessary cookies, such as a load-balancer session cookie or a security/anti-abuse cookie, to deliver pages to your browser. These cookies are managed by the host (currently GitHub, Inc.) under its own privacy policy. We do not read or share them.

3. Embedded content

This site loads web fonts from a public font CDN (Google Fonts) so headings render in Source Serif 4 and Inter. The font request may include your IP address and User-Agent in the font CDN's request logs under its own terms. We do not receive that information. We do not embed YouTube, Vimeo, social-media widgets, or third-party comment systems on these pages.

4. Do Not Track / Global Privacy Control

Because we do not run analytics, advertising, or personalization on this site, there is nothing here to honor or ignore a DNT or GPC signal against. For in-app data, where applicable, we honor the Universal Opt-Out Mechanism signals (including Global Privacy Control) as required by California, Colorado, and Connecticut law.

5. Changes

If we ever add analytics or a content-management tool that uses cookies, we will update this page first and add a banner before any non-essential cookie is set.
