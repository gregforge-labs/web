Privacy Policy — Jyotish Vani

Last updated: April 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Gregforge Labs LLC ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the Jyotish Vani mobile application (the "App"). By using the App you agree to this policy.

1. Who we are

Gregforge Labs LLC, a California limited liability company (Entity #B20260047024), 675 Tasman Drive, Apt 2314, Sunnyvale, CA 94089, United States. Contact: nikhila@gregforgelabs.com.

2. Information we collect

We collect only what is needed to operate the App. We do not sell your data.

  • Account information. Email address and password (the password is handled by Firebase Authentication and is never visible to us in plaintext).
  • Birth profile. Name, date of birth, time of birth, place of birth (with derived latitude, longitude, and timezone), gender, and (optional) current location and occupation. You provide this so the App can compute your astrological chart.
  • Chat history. Messages you send to the in-app astrology assistant ("Vani") and the assistant's replies. Stored locally on your device for conversation continuity. The current message and recent context are sent to our backend service for each turn.
  • Device and diagnostic information. Generic technical data (app version, OS version, anonymized error reports). We do not collect advertising identifiers, contacts, photos, microphone, camera, or precise location.

3. How we use information

  • To create and authenticate your account.
  • To compute your Vedic astrological chart and personalized interpretations.
  • To deliver answers from the in-app assistant.
  • To diagnose crashes and improve reliability.
  • To comply with applicable laws.

4. Service providers (sub-processors)

We use a small number of trusted providers to operate the App. They process information only on our behalf and only for the purposes described here.

  • Google Firebase Authentication — manages your email/password login.
  • Google Cloud Run (United States) — hosts our backend chat service.
  • Anthropic, PBC — provides the large language model that powers the assistant. Your message and the relevant context are sent to Anthropic's API to generate each reply. Anthropic does not use this content to train its models.
  • Google Maps Platform (Places & Time Zone APIs) — used to look up the city you typed during onboarding and to resolve its timezone.

5. Where data is stored

Your birth profile and chat history are stored locally on your device. Account credentials are stored by Firebase Authentication on Google's servers in the United States. Each chat message you send is processed in transit through our Cloud Run service in the United States and Anthropic's API; we do not retain chat content on our servers after the response is returned.

6. Data retention

  • Account. Retained as long as you maintain an account. You may delete your account at any time (see Section 8); deletion removes your Firebase authentication record.
  • Birth profile and chat history. Stored on your device. Removed when you delete the App or clear app data.
  • Backend logs. Operational logs (timestamps, error traces, anonymized request metadata) are retained for up to 30 days for debugging and abuse prevention.

7. Children

The App is not directed to children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact us and we will delete it.

8. Your choices and rights

  • Access, correction, deletion. You can edit your birth profile in the App at any time. To delete your account and associated authentication record, email nikhila@gregforgelabs.com from the address you registered with.
  • California residents (CCPA/CPRA). You have the right to know what personal information we collect, request deletion, and not be discriminated against for exercising these rights. To exercise them, contact us as above.
  • EEA / UK residents (GDPR). You have rights of access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, and objection. The lawful basis for processing your information is the contract you enter into by using the App, and our legitimate interest in operating the service.

9. Security

We use industry-standard safeguards including TLS encryption for data in transit, Google Cloud's encryption at rest for stored credentials, and least-privilege access controls. No system is perfectly secure; we encourage you to use a strong, unique password.
