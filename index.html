Inside the app

Not a wall of tables. A book about you.

  • Your day, from your chart

    Open the app each morning to a short reading written from your own chart — what the day favors, what to hold back on, and which hours matter. Week and month views look further ahead.

  • A kundli of 22 chapters

    Your birth chart opens as a book about you — who you are, what you carry, and where the years ahead point. Twenty-two chapters in plain language, as deep as you want to go.

  • Panchang for your city

    The Hindu almanac, computed for where you actually live — Hyderabad or Houston. Good hours, festival days, fasting days, and the windows to avoid.

  • Is now a good time?

    A simple verdict for this hour, updated live. Planning something bigger — a ceremony, a journey, a signing? The muhurat finder scans the weeks ahead for the cleanest window.

  • Matching, honestly told

    Checking a match for marriage? Get the full picture — all 36 points, the strengths, the frictions, and what they actually mean. A verdict your family can talk about, not just a number.

  • The next five years

    Career, money, home, relationships — a five-year forecast mapped from your chart's own timing, told in plain guidance rather than jargon.
