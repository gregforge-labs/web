Questions

Asked before you ask.

How accurate are the calculations?

Planetary positions are computed by the Swiss Ephemeris — the professional standard used across serious astrology software — under a commercial Professional Edition license. Charts, dashas, and panchang are calculated for your exact birth time and place, not looked up in tables.

How is Vani different from consulting a jyotishi?

Vani answers from your own chart and running dasha, names the classical rule behind each answer, and is available whenever a question occurs to you — no appointment, no per-minute charges. It is a companion for reflection, not a replacement for human judgment on big decisions.

Is my birth data private?

Yes. You sign in with just a phone number — no email, no password. Your birth details are used to compute your chart and readings, are never sold, and you can delete your account and every trace of your data at any time, from inside the app or from this site. Details in the Privacy Policy.

What does it cost?

Casting your kundli, daily readings, panchang, and matching are free. Two optional monthly plans — Pro (from ₹399/month) and Max (from ₹799/month), priced per market — expand your Vani allowance, remove ads, and open every chapter. Cancel anytime in your store settings.

Available in 14 countries: India, Nepal, Sri Lanka, Malaysia, Mauritius, New Zealand, the United States, the United Kingdom, Canada, Australia, the UAE, Saudi Arabia, Qatar, and Singapore.
